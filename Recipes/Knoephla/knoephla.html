Knoephla Soup

Description

Growing up in North Dakota, I subsisted off of knoephla soup. It is a thicker creamy soup based on chicken stock and cream.

Ingredients

  • 5 raw potatoes, diced
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 stalk celery, finely chopped
  • 1 gallon water
  • 2 tablespoons chicken base
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 cup cream
  • 3 tablespoons butter

Steps

  1. In large soup pot, combine potatoes, onion, celery, water, and chicken base. Cook until potatoes are soft.
  2. For the dough: combine flour, baking powder, salt, and eggs with 1/2 cup of water
  3. Roll dough into log shapes approximately 1/2 inch in diameter.
  4. Cut 1/2 inch pieces of the log into the hot soup mixture
  5. Add cream of chicken soup, cream, and butter
  6. Simmer. Soup is finished when the knoephlas are soft (12-15 mins)
